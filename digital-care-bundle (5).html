Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these courses CPD accredited?

Yes. All 11 courses in the bundle are CPD accredited and comply with CQC and ICO requirements for health and social care training in the UK.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes. On successful completion of each course you'll receive an instant digital CPD certificate, downloadable as a PDF to add to your records or staff file.

How long do I have access?

You have 365 days of access from the date of purchase. All courses are self-paced — start and stop whenever suits you.

Can I access on mobile or tablet?

Absolutely. All courses are fully responsive and work on any device — smartphone, tablet, laptop, or desktop. Learn anywhere, anytime.

Is there an assessment?

Yes, each course ends with a short multiple-choice assessment. You can retake it unlimited times at no extra cost until you pass and receive your certificate.

Can my employer purchase for the whole team?

Yes. We offer team licences (up to 10 staff) and unlimited organisational licences with a manager dashboard and bulk certificate downloads. See our pricing plans above.

Is this training CQC compliant?

Yes. Course content is aligned with CQC Fundamental Standards, UK GDPR, the Data Protection Act 2018, and NHS Digital's data security guidance.

Can I buy individual courses instead?

Yes. Every course is available to purchase individually. The bundle simply gives you all 11 at a 33% saving compared to buying them separately.
