Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Are these courses CPD accredited?

Yes. The courses are designed for CPD learning and aligned with common CQC, ICO, and Skills for Care expectations for UK health and social care training.

Why were Safeguarding and Mental Health First Aid added?

Both are high-priority topics in many UK care settings. We added them because employers often ask for stronger safeguarding and mental health awareness evidence during onboarding, supervision, and audit preparation.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes. You receive an instant digital CPD certificate on completion of each course, downloadable as a PDF for your employment records or CQC staff files.

How long do I have access?

365 days from the date of purchase. All courses are fully self-paced — start and stop whenever suits you, with no time pressure.

Can I access on mobile or tablet?

Absolutely. All courses are fully responsive and work on any device — smartphone, tablet, laptop, or desktop. Learn anywhere, anytime.

Can my employer buy for the whole team?

Yes. Team licences cover up to 10 staff (£379), while organisation licences (£849) include certificate and transcript options for 15 team members plus lifetime course access.

Is this CQC aligned?

The content is built to support CQC inspection readiness and is aligned with relevant guidance including UK GDPR, the Data Protection Act 2018, the Care Act 2014, and NHS Digital data security principles.

Can I buy individual courses instead?

Yes — every course is available individually. The bundle saves you 35% versus buying separately, and the custom builder lets you pick any combination with automatic discounts.
